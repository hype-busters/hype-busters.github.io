Best-Worst Scaling Tool

A methodology for measuring preferences and perceptions in scientific language.

What is Best-Worst Scaling?

Best-Worst Scaling (BWS) asks participants to identify the "best" and "worst" items from a set. This forces choices between items, providing more reliable data than traditional rating scales.
